  • Countdown

    The idea for this project came after watching 8 out of 10 Cats does countdown and failing miserably at getting anywhere near a respectable word count. What do you do as a software engineer??     A game that works out what darts are available for you to throw to compete a 501 game of darts. Always finishing on a double. There is an interface to select the darts you throw to keep track of your score.
