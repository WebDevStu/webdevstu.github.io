Who are you?

Hello, I'm Stewart Anderson, aged 36, I live in York, North Yorkshire with my wife and two children. I'm a Software Engineer for a Network Security Company. Born in Scotland, I've lived in England the majority of my life, pursuing a career in development for the web.

Outside of the web world, I'm a keen Amateur Photographer, you can find me at 11:30pm taking pictures of the stars in my back garden some nights. My other main interest is Cars, a through and through petrol head, I currently have a 1984 Ford Capri in my garage, that I'm (very) slowly restoring to former glory. I'm never happier than when I'm photographing cars at shows throughout the country, just need to finish my car so I can show it off.
